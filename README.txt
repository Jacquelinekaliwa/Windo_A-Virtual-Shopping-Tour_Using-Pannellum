##ABOUT PROJECT
With the rapid growth of digital technologies, virtual tours have become an increasingly popular way for users to explore locations remotely. They offer immersive experiences that provide a sense of presence and interactivity, replicating the feeling of being physically present in a space. In shopping districts, virtual tours can revolutionize the way customers interact with stores by providing detailed visual information and interactive navigation before visiting in person. This project aims to address the lack of accessible virtual tours for shopping areas in Lilongwe, Malawi as well as bring light to the possibilities  of such an amazing feature. 

It was a mini-project to fulfill academic reasons. Due to time and no access to take pictures of inside the store I could only manage outside a store and a bar.
